Hi, I'm Dr. Raj Kumar Mishra

I am Dr. Raj Kumar Mishra, a committed healthcare professional focused on delivering exceptional patient care and setting high standards in medical practice. My journey began with a BAMS from SAC Lucknow, where I cultivated a strong foundation in medicine and developed a profound commitment to healing and service. From the very start, I have been driven by a deep-rooted passion for helping others and enhancing healthcare standards.

Throughout my career, I have embraced opportunities to work in diverse healthcare settings, allowing me to gain comprehensive experience across various medical domains. I’ve served in leadership roles at multiple prestigious institutions, where I managed critical care, led emergency response teams, and contributed to effective patient outcomes. My work has not only reinforced my expertise but has also strengthened my resolve to make a meaningful impact in the medical field.

I am dedicated to advancing healthcare through compassionate patient care, strategic leadership, and a continuous drive to improve medical practices and accessibility. My work is a blend of hands-on patient care and administrative leadership, aimed at creating a healthcare environment that prioritizes both innovation and empathy. As I continue my journey, I remain focused on my mission to bridge gaps in healthcare accessibility and to make a lasting, positive impact on patients’ lives.

Education & Experience

Education at SAC Lucknow

My journey in medicine began with a Bachelor of Ayurvedic Medicine and Surgery (BAMS) from SAC Lucknow, where I graduated in 2003. This education laid a solid foundation in both traditional and modern medical practices, shaping my approach to holistic and patient-centered care. I developed a deep commitment to healing and a profound respect for the field of medicine.

2003

Bir Tikendrajit University, Manipur

I hold an MBA in Hospital Administration from Bir Tikendrajit University, Manipur, completed with first-division honors. This qualification has expanded my expertise in healthcare management, focusing on strategic planning, hospital operations, and patient care optimization. With this blend of medical and administrative skills, I am equipped to enhance healthcare systems by balancing clinical insight with effective management practices.

Vivekanand Polyclinic and Institute of Medical Sciences, Lucknow

As a Hospital Administrator and in-charge of casualty at an NABH-accredited hospital, I was responsible for managing medical and surgical emergencies, ensuring that patients received immediate and effective care. Leading the casualty department, I handled critical cases with precision and compassion, often working under intense conditions. My time here reinforced my crisis management skills and my ability to make swift, life-saving decisions. This role strengthened my leadership skills as I coordinated with teams to streamline emergency response protocols.

2004 - 2012

Mayo Medical College and Hospital, Barabanki

I joined Mayo Medical College and Hospital as the Chief Medical Officer (CMO), where I focused on enhancing patient care and improving operational efficiency. My role involved overseeing patient treatment plans, coordinating with department heads, and implementing policies to optimize medical procedures. Here, I emphasized team collaboration and set high standards for quality care, contributing to the hospital’s reputation for excellence.

2012-14

TSM Medical College and Hospital

At TSM Medical College, which is NABH accredited, I held the dual responsibilities of Chief Medical Officer and in-charge of the casualty and ICU departments. During the COVID-19 crisis, I closely monitored critical cases, leading efforts to ensure patient safety and manage ICU capacity effectively. My role demanded resilience and adaptability, especially as we faced unprecedented challenges due to the pandemic.

2014-2020

LBS Mahila Ayurvedic College and Hospital, Bilaspur, Haryana

Serving as Deputy Medical Superintendent and Chief Physician at LBS Mahila Ayurvedic College allowed me to combine my clinical expertise with administrative responsibilities. I supervised patient care, guided junior physicians, and introduced initiatives to streamline hospital operations. My role focused on improving patient experiences and outcomes, ensuring that our practices aligned with both modern medical standards and traditional Ayurvedic principles.

December 2019 - May 2021

RGS Ayurvedic Medical College & Research Centre

In my current position at RGS Hospital, I initially joined as Deputy Medical Superintendent and have since been promoted to Medical Superintendent. Here, I am entrusted with overseeing hospital operations, leading medical teams, and ensuring compliance with healthcare standards. My focus is on improving patient outcomes, optimizing hospital workflow, and fostering a culture of empathy and excellence among our staff. As Medical Superintendent, I work closely with department heads to implement best practices and continuously elevate the standard of care provided at RGS Hospital. I am dedicated to leading the hospital toward growth and innovation, driven by a commitment to making a lasting difference in patient care.

November 2021 - Present
